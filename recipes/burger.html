Best Hamburger

This hamburger has carmalized onions and bacon bits emulsified in a blue cheese aoili. The burger is garnished with fresh aresian blue cheese, pickled red onions, and butter lettuce.

Ingrediants

  • Sour Dough Hamburger Bun
  • 80/20 Certified Angus Beef Hamburger Patty
  • Artesian Blue Cheese Spread
  • Red onion
  • Bacon
  • Duke's Mayo
  • Butter Lettuce

Steps

  1. Fry up some bacon of your choice
  2. Chop finely when done cooking
  3. Chop 1/2 of a red onion for carmelization, take large slices with the other half for pickling
  4. Carmalize Onions
  5. Pickle Onions
    • Note it might be easier to buy these at the store
    • This is a highly reviewed picked onion recipe
  6. Cook hamburger patty
  7. While burger cooks make aoli
    • 1/3 cup mayo
    • Add carmalized onions and chopped bacon to mayo
    • Add artesian blue cheese to taste
  8. When there is ~3 minutes left on the burger coat one side with the artesian blue cheese
  9. Flip burger, apply blue cheese, turn off heat and cover the patty
  10. Toast sourdough buns in carmalized onion pan
  11. Assembly
    • Bottom bun
    • Burger Patty
    • Butter lettuce
    • Picked Onion
    • Onion, bacon, bluecheese aoli spread on top bun
    • Place top bun and enjoy!
