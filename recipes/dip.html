Spinich Artichoke Dip

This dip has sausage, cream cheese, spinich, artichoke, sundried tomatos, and various spices.

Ingrediants

  • 2 blocks of cream cheese
  • 1 lb sausage
  • 1 cup sundried tomatos
  • 1 cup canned artichoke
  • 2 cups frozen spinich
  • 1 tbsp each:
    • Basil
    • Oregano
    • Thyme
    • Rosemary
    • Salt & pepper
    • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook sausage
  2. Mix all ingrediants into baking dish
  3. Bake at 325 F for 25 minutes
