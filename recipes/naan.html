Naan Nachos

This recipe includes mostly fresh ingrediants. It's fresh but full tastiness is sure to satisfy.

Ingrediants

  • 2 pieces naan bread
  • 1 cup tzaziki
  • 1 cup of shreded chicken
  • Chopped:
    • Red onion
    • Baby tomatoes
    • Avocado
    • Cucumber
  • Oil
  • Salt & pepper

Steps

  1. Slice naan into pizza shape
  2. Cut veggies & place on naan
  3. Cover in tzaziki and feta
